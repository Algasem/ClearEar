Hearing Test
Calibrate ClearEar to your hearing
What this tests

Pure-tone audiometry across 8 frequencies. We'll play tones at decreasing volumes until you can barely hear them. This maps your hearing threshold at each frequency.

How to prepare

Use headphones or earbuds in a quiet room. You'll hear a tone and press "I hear it" or "I don't hear it." The test takes about 3 minutes.
